About Junk2CLEAN

We're more than just a junk removal service – we're your neighbors, committed to keeping the desert beautiful while providing exceptional, eco-conscious service.

Our Story

Junk2CLEAN was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: make junk removal easy, transparent, and environmentally responsible. We saw too many companies prioritizing speed over sustainability, dumping everything into landfills without a second thought.

We knew there was a better way. A way that respects our desert environment, supports local charities, and gives our customers peace of mind knowing their unwanted items are being handled responsibly.

Today, we're proud to have diverted thousands of tons from landfills through recycling and donation programs, all while providing same-day service and transparent pricing to the Phoenix metro area.

Our Mission & Values

We're guided by principles that put people and the planet first.

Eco-Conscious

We prioritize recycling and donation over landfills. Every item we collect is evaluated for reuse, recycling, or responsible disposal. Our goal is to keep 75% or more of collected items out of landfills.

Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees, no surprise charges. We provide upfront, volume-based pricing and always confirm the cost before we start loading. What we quote is what you pay.

Customer First

We treat every customer like family. From the first call to the final cleanup, we're professional, punctual, and respectful. Your satisfaction is our top priority.

Licensed & Insured

Full liability insurance and proper licensing give you peace of mind. We're bonded, insured, and operate with complete transparency and accountability.

Always On Time

We respect your time. Our 30-minute arrival windows and real-time updates mean you'll never be left waiting. Same-day and next-day service available.

Community Focused

We partner with local charities and donation centers. Your gently used furniture, appliances, and household items help families in need across the Phoenix metro area.
