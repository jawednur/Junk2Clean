Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to move items to the curb?
No! Our team handles all the heavy lifting. We'll remove items from anywhere on your property - garage, backyard, upstairs, basement - you name it. Just point us in the right direction.
What items can you not take?
We cannot accept hazardous materials including paint, chemicals, fuel, asbestos, or pressurized tanks. We're happy to recommend specialized disposal services for these items.
How do I get an exact price?
Send us a few photos when you request a quote for the most accurate estimate, or we can provide a free on-site assessment with no obligation to book.
How quickly can you come?
We offer same-day and next-day service in most areas. During busy seasons, we typically book within 48 hours. Emergency cleanouts are available.
