What Affects Pricing?

Our transparent pricing model considers several factors to give you the fairest rate:

Volume

The amount of space your items take up in our truck is the primary factor. We measure in truck loads: minimum, quarter, half, or full.

Weight

Heavy items like concrete, dirt, or dense materials may affect pricing due to disposal costs and labor requirements.

Accessibility

Items on ground level are standard. Stairs, long carries, or difficult access may require additional time and labor.

Item Type

Some items like mattresses, tires, or appliances have special disposal requirements that may affect pricing.
